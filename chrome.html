Google Chrome

Google Chrome is one of the most popular browsers. Created by Google, it has many functionalities which tie into Google Devices and Google Accounts. The monopoly Google has makes this browser a solid pick for most people.

Google Chrome comes with many features - integration with Google Accounts, data syncing, and many more - which makes this an ideal browser for the average user.


Did you know?

Chrome or Chromium comes default on many of Google's devices.
